Chat

The core of the app is a conversational UI, where two bot personalities meet.

Staty is responsible for everything that is related to gathering data. He will ask you about how did your workout go, how much did you lift today on the bench press.

And Coach who is responsible for pushing you towards your goals, nagging you to go to the Gym, scheduling your week combined with your private calendar.


Staty occasionally asks for selfies of you after workout when you get home, this way he can show you your progress on the way.
While Coach can explain you certain exercises.
.

Statistics

Staty's territory. This is a card-based view, where you get exciting feedbacks about how you proceed.

This is also the place where your profile lives. You can set up all your data here, but also connect your smart health devices so we get more accurate inputs.


You can compare your performance over time, what Staty calculates based on the times you spent at your Gym, but also on your input after a workouts.


And you can see how your body reacts, especially if you have a smart scale, what you can connect to Staty

Schedule

Coach's territory. You can start a program here, or check the schedule if you already started one.

You can select any of these programs:

And You will get a scheduled timeline following the goal of the program.

The schedule will be automatically reorganized if you ask Coach to reschedule a session (skip a day), or he finds a conflicting event in your calendar. He will also try to find the optional time to go to Gym considering how busy the place it at a time and how much time does it take to get there.
